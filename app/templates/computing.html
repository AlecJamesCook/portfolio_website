MSc Computing

This page lays out the details of the MSc programme that I am currently enrolled at. Cardiff University's MSc Computing is a conversion course designed for people from non-computer science programmes like myself. I hope that by taking a look at the modules that I will have to complete by the end of the programme, you will be able to get a little feel of the intensity of the course and the breadth and depth of the topics that I will be utilising in my future work. Each module outline comes with a description, a list of key skills taught in the module, and the ways in which we are assessed
